Krowi's Brokers [Currency] is a DataBroker addon that displays your gold and currencies in a clean, customizable way. Track gold across all characters, monitor session earnings, and organize currencies exactly how you want them.

### Display Modes
Choose what appears on your data broker bar:
- **Character Gold**  Current character's money only.
- **Current Faction Total**  All Alliance or Horde characters combined.
- **Realm Total**  Everything on your current realm.
- **Account Total**  All characters across all realms plus Warband Bank.
- **Warband Bank**  Shared account bank balance only.

### Tooltip Views
Switch between three tooltip styles with modifier keys:
- **Money Tooltip**  All characters listed with their gold, sorted by wealth. Includes faction totals, session tracking, and WoW Token price (optional).
- **Currency Tooltip**  All currencies organized by expansion or type.
- **Combined Tooltip**  Both money and currencies in one view.

Hold **Shift** or **Ctrl** while hovering to cycle between tooltips. The hints update based on your default setting.

### Session Tracking
Watch your gold changes in real-time:
- Track for 1 to 48 hours with automatic extension while you play.
- Shows earned and spent amounts in the tooltip.
- Updates instantly with loot, trades, repairs, mail, and vendor purchases.
- Survives reloads and disconnects.
- Toggle off to clear and reset.

### Character Overview
All your characters at a glance:
- Listed in the money tooltip, sorted by wealth.
- Class colors for easy identification.
- Green indicator marks your active character.
- Cross-realm support with realm names shown when needed.
- Separate faction totals for Alliance and Horde.
- Warband Bank included in account totals.

### Money Options
- **Labels**  None, text (g/s/c), or icon.
- **Abbreviation**  None, 1k, or 1m format.
- **Separators**  Space, period, or comma for thousands.
- **Gold Only**  Hide silver and copper completely.
- **Colored Coins**  Toggle gold/silver/copper colors.
- **Character Limit**  Display 5 to 30 characters in tooltips.
- **WoW Token**  Show current market price in money tooltip.

### Currency Options
- **Group by Header**  Organize currencies by expansion or type.
- **Hide Unused**  Only show currencies you have.
- **Abbreviation**  None, 1k, or 1m format.
- **Header Visibility**  Show or hide specific currency groups.
- **Alphabetical Sorting**  Currencies sorted by name within each group.

### Configuration
- **Default Tooltip**  Choose which tooltip shows by default.
- **Realm Tracking**  Track all realms or current realm only.
- **Session Duration**  Select from 1, 2, 4, 8, 12, 24, or 48 hours.
- **Activity Check**  Configurable interval to extend active sessions.
- Right-click the broker button to access all settings quickly.
